    Commonwealth Ministries devolved ministerial departments of Vekllei. They are lead by a qualified member of the Cabinet appointed by the Directory.

    Each ministry is lead by a ministerial secretariat, which centralises authority and policy across the constituent commonwealths. Each constituent has the same ten ministries, which are coordinated centrally by the secretariat.

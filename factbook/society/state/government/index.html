    The Commonwealth Government is the national government of Vekllei and is a federal, parliamentary constitutional republic. It consists of several parts:

    Executive

    A supreme executive of all Commonwealth republics and constituents called the Council, consisting of

    • The Directory, a council of Sorda
    • The Cabinet

    Legislature

    A federal legislature, including

    • A federal Senate
    • Regional lower houses, called a National Assemblies

    Public Administration

    Sectors of public administration, including

    • Federal parliaments
    • Constituent ministries

    Commonwealth Parliaments

    • Community Parliament
    • Civic Parliament
    • Democracy Council
    • Health Parliament
    • Education Parliament
    • Security Parliament

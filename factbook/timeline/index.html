    Timeline of Atlantic History

    This timeline is a useful curated reference of Atlantic history, but is not comprehensive. It is continuously evolving with this site.

    Pre-War Timeline #

    1. 6000BC Earliest evidence of human settlement in Oslola
    2. 1230BC Irish settlers establish fishing communities on south coast of Oslola
    3. 1100BC Scandinavians settle in Smoke Cove, aka Lola
    4. 600BC Oslola is politically and culturally isolated from Europe
    5. 400BC Oslola is using Oslolan logographs
      Upen is being practiced
    6. 500BC Beginning of Oslolan Common Period
    7. 200 Beginning of Oslolan Early Period
    8. 230 Language reforms are initiated by Queen Souisviasn
    9. 700 Beginning of Oslolan Middle Period
      Founding of the 1st Commonwealth
      Oslola’s monarchist period
    10. 900 Golden age of sagas begins
    11. 1100 Helvasia is discovered by Scandinavians, who observe Arctic Sami living there
      Anguilla and Antigua are settled by Arawaks
    12. 1200 Founding of the 2nd Commonwealth
      Aismo Desimou becomes king
      Capital moved to Altanehs
      Arawaks are displaced by Caribs in Antigua
      Irish settlers establish Aran on Demon
    13. 1350 Rise of trading towns on East Coast
    14. 1380 Navy coup forces monarchy from power
    15. 1350 Rise of trading towns on East Coast
    16. 1400 Beginning of Period of Rest
      Founding of the 3rd Commonwealth
      Collapse of Oslolan trading power begins
    17. 1440 Kala enters a union with the 3rd Commonwealth
    18. 1455 Costa Verde is discovered and settled by Portugal
    19. 1492 Antigua is discovered by Europeans
    20. 1600 Beginning of Vekllei Late Period
      Oslola loses the Six Years War to the British Empire and is capitulated as a colony of the British Empire.
    21. 1642 Commonwealth Bank is founded in Oslola as the Royal Bank
    22. 1643 Helvasia is rediscovered by Europeans and subsequently settled by whalers.
    23. 1689 Allia and Aloi are settled by the United Kingdom
    24. 1692 Barbary is founded as a slave estate known as Codrington's Island
    25. 1695 Antigua and Barbados are settled by the United Kingdom
      Combermere School opens in Barbados
    26. 1815 Ascension is settled by the United Kingdom
    27. 1830 Atlantic is founded in the Oslola
    28. 1836 The British Overseas Territories consisting of Oslola, Kala and Demon are reincorporated as the British Atlantic Territories, including Anguilla, Bermuda, the British Virgin Islands, the Cayman Islands, the Falkland Islands, Montserrat, the Saint Helena, Ascension, and Tristan de Cuna Islands, South Georgia and Sandwich Islands, and the Turks and Caicos Islands.
    29. 1838 The new British Atlantic Territories are granted an executive governor, essentially affording self-governing status to the colonies.
    30. 1841 The Ginnery is founded in Barbary
    31. 1842 The Atlantic daily newspaper is founded
      The Great Recession hits the metropole
    32. 1849 Royal University of the West Indies is founded in Antigua
    33. 1874 Montre University is founded
    34. 1899 Gina is founded in Oslola
    35. 1901 The British Atlantic Territories are granted an
    36. 1902 Cateral is founded in Kairi as Cateral Artillery Co.
    37. 1908 The future capital of Godthul is founded as a whaling station by Norway in Sude
    39. 1925 Rail transport in Oslola is nationalised
    40. 1926 The British Atlantic Territories are awarded Dominion status at the 1926 Imperial Conference, granting it autonomy from British administration.
    41. 1935 Beginning of the Late Commonwealth
      The Atlantic Independence Movement culminates in Oslola achieving independence from the UK, retaining Edward VII as the head of state.
    42. 1933 Sugarhouse Mountain Fortress begins construction outside Montre
      U.K. deploys frigates to the North Atlantic
    43. 1940 The Second World War begins
    44. 1943 The assassination of Adolf Hitler ends the Second World War and results in the partition of Germany
      The United Nations is founded
    45. 1946 Nuclear weapons used by the US against Japan
    46. 1946 American-Japanese War ends
    47. 1963 Oslola becomes independent of the British Crown, replacing the monarch with the Atlantic Council
      Costa Verde along with the rest of the Azores votes to secede from Portugal after the recession worsens
    48. 1966 United Kingdom enters decolonisation talks in Edinburgh Conference
      The Imperial Trade House in Oslola is burnt down by arsonists
    49. 1968 Founding of the First Commonwealth of Oceans
      The United Kingdom grants independence to the British Atlantic Territories, which chooses to remain a single country after a referendum. The new country is known as the Atlantic Federation
    50. 1976 Costa Verde accedes to the Atlantic Federation
    51. 1978 The 1978 World’s Fair is held in Montre
      1978 World's Fair terror attack kills 2,300 people
      Atlantic Crown dissolves under republican pressure
    52. 1986 Government is sacked by the Governor-General after corruption inquiry
      Constitutional crisis begins
      Martial Law is declared in Vekllei
    53. 1989 UK frigate sunk after tensions over VK-Faroese corridor access
    54. 1992 Oslolan Civil War begins along political and ethnic lines
    55. 1993 The Atlantic Crown junta collapses but the rebellion is unable to consolidate control, leading to further fighting
    56. 1995 First Atomic War
      100 Days Grief
      Vekllei is occupied by the UK & the US
      The foundation of the European Economic Community (EEC)
      The US establishes a lunar colony in Ocean of Storms
    57. 2004 Wallabies are introduced to Vekllei
      British Gambia border skirmish occurs
      The Senegalese Crisis begins
      British expeditionary forces invade Senegal
    58. 2006 The USSR establishes a lunar colony in Eastern Sea
      Moon I treaty establishes protected Lunar Nullius
      The PRC establishes a lunar colony on East Equator
